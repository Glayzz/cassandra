Questions

Good to know.

Is it really free?
Yes. Cassandra is a free A2MCP service for the OKX.AI Genesis Hackathon and beyond. No API key on your side, no signup, no gas. You send an address; she sends a prophecy.
Do you need my wallet or private keys?
Never. Every read is on public on-chain data. You paste a public address or an unsigned transaction blob. There's nothing to connect and nothing to leak — the whole service is stateless.
Which chains are supported?
Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, BNB Chain and Solana today, selected with a chain parameter. EVM chains resolve through Etherscan v2, which covers 50+ networks — adding more is a one-line change.
How is this different from Revoke.cash or a wallet's built-in warning?
Those show you a list or a generic flag. Cassandra narrates the specific counterfactual consequence, prices live capped exposure, traces a token deployer's history, detects same-person wallets, and grades a whole wallet in one call — across EVM and Solana, and callable by AI agents.
What is A2MCP?
Agent-to-MCP: a standardized way for AI agents to call a service as a tool over the Model Context Protocol. Cassandra serves MCP at /mcp, so any A2MCP agent can install and use her with no custom integration.
Can I use it without an agent?
Absolutely. Every oracle has a plain JSON REST route (/foresee/…) and the interactive playground lets you try all five with one click.
